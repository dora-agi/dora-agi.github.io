关于我们 About Us

我们的故事 Our Story

成都多纳科技有限公司成立于2025年4月16日，我们专注于利用尖端技术，特别是人工智能，来解决现实世界的问题并创造有价值的解决方案。 Founded on April 16, 2025, Chengdu Dora Technology Co., Ltd. focuses on leveraging cutting-edge technology, especially artificial intelligence, to solve real-world problems and create valuable solutions.

我们的团队由经验丰富的专业人士组成，对创新充满热情，致力于为客户提供高质量的软件产品和服务。 Our team consists of experienced professionals passionate about innovation and dedicated to delivering high-quality software products and services to our clients.

主营业务 Our Main Business

软件开发 Software Development

我们提供定制化的软件开发服务，涵盖Web应用、移动应用以及企业级解决方案。我们的开发流程注重效率和质量，确保交付卓越的产品。 We offer customized software development services, including web applications, mobile apps, and enterprise solutions. Our development process emphasizes efficiency and quality to ensure the delivery of exceptional products.

软件销售 Software Sales

我们不仅开发软件，也提供成熟的软件产品销售。我们的产品旨在帮助企业提高效率、优化流程并实现业务增长。 We not only develop software but also offer mature software products for sale. Our products are designed to help businesses improve efficiency, optimize processes, and achieve growth.
